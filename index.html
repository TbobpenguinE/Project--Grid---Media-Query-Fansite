Step into the World of Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V

Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V follows Yuya Sakaki, an Entertainment Duelist who wants to make people smile with his duels. Along the way, he learns about Pendulum Summoning, interdimensional conflict, and the truth behind the four separate dimensions linked by Duel Monsters.

What Is ARC-V About?

The story begins in the Standard Dimension, where Yuya studies at You Show Duel School and dreams of becoming a great entertainer like his father. His life changes when a high-stakes Duel gives him the chance to show off a new summoning method: Pendulum Summoning.

As the series goes on, the cast is pulled into a conflict that spans the Standard, Synchro, Xyz, and Fusion dimensions. Friends and rivals from each world collide, and duels become a way to fight for freedom, identity, and what “smiles” actually mean.

  • Genre: Shōnen / Action / Fantasy
  • Focus: Duel Monsters, Pendulum Summoning, Dimension Travel
  • Vibes: Over-the-top duels, found family, emotional dragons

ARC-V Quick Facts

Main Protagonist Yuya Sakaki
Number of Episodes 148
Original Run 2014–2017
Signature Mechanic Pendulum Summoning

ARC-V mixes classic Yu-Gi-Oh! duel hype with new mechanics and the idea that dueling is a performance meant to bring joy, even when the stakes get absurdly high.

Why Watch ARC-V?

ARC-V is packed with creative duel setups, overdramatic speeches, and big emotional swings involving dragons, friends, and morally questionable adults. If you like card games that behave like full-on boss battles, this series is very much that.
