Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V follows Yuya Sakaki, an Entertainment Duelist who wants to make people smile with his duels. As the story goes on, he gets pulled into a conflict that spans multiple dimensions.

About the Show

ARC-V starts in the Standard Dimension, where Yuya studies at You Show Duel School. After showing off Pendulum Summoning, he becomes the center of a much larger story.

Different dimensions focus on different summoning styles and different rules. The characters have to figure out what “entertainment” means when duels suddenly have real consequences.

  • Genre: Shōnen / Action / Fantasy
  • Main character: Yuya Sakaki
  • Key mechanic: Pendulum Summoning
  • Running time: 148 episodes

Quick Facts

  • Goal: Make people smile with duels
  • Dimensions: Standard, Synchro, Xyz, Fusion
  • Theme: Identity, performance, and choice

ARC-V is known for flashy duels, emotional dragons, and the idea that a duel can be both a show and a serious fight at the same time.

Why Watch ARC-V?

If you like Yu-Gi-Oh! in general, ARC-V adds:

  1. New duel mechanics and crazy Action Duels.
  2. Multiple versions of “Yuya” from different worlds.
  3. A plot that slowly reveals how all the dimensions connect.
