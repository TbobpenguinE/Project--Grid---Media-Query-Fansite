Characters and Scenes

Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V has bold colors, loud duel fields, and character designs that lean into “maximum dramatic hair”. This page shows a few important faces and places.

Yuya Sakaki

The main character. An Entertainment Duelist who wants to make people smile, even when things get serious, stubborn one.

Zuzu / Yuzu

Yuya’s friend who backs him up in duels and arguments. Known for her strong sense of justice (and her paper fan).

Major Antagonist

ARC-V has several “villain” figures, from CEOs to invaders from other dimensions. Many of them have their own reasons. This one's the CEO type.

You Show Duel School

A small, “weak-looking” duel school that actually encourages fun and creativity, being more of a hub for outcasts in contrast to LID. It feels like home for the main cast.

Emotional Moment

The show likes to drop big emotional scenes in the middle of duels. Tears, shouting, and card effects all at once... they are playing a card game for your information.

Action Duel

Action Duels mix running, jumping, and grabbing Action Cards from the field with normal card game strategy.

Visual Details to Watch

  • How each dimension uses color to show its mood.
  • How Yuya’s roster of dragons change as the story goes on.
  • Posters and signs in the background of city scenes.
  • Differences between “fun” duels and war-like duels, because it's war.

Even if you ignore the card text, you can follow a lot of the story just by watching colors, expressions, and posing, not surprising.
