Characters and Scenes

Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V has bold colors, loud duel fields, and character designs that lean into “maximum dramatic hair”. This page shows a few important faces and places.

You can replace these placeholder images with real screenshots later, like Yuya, Yuzu, Reiji, the school, and your favorite duel moments.

Yuya Sakaki

The main character. An Entertainment Duelist who wants to make people smile, even when things get serious.

Yuzu / Zuzu

Yuya’s friend who backs him up in duels and arguments. Known for her strong sense of justice (and her paper fan).

Major Antagonist

ARC-V has several “villain” figures, from CEOs to invaders from other dimensions. Many of them have their own reasons.

You Show Duel School

A small, “weak-looking” duel school that actually encourages fun and creativity. It feels like home for the main cast.

Emotional Moment

The show likes to drop big emotional scenes in the middle of duels. Tears, shouting, and card effects all at once.

Action Duel

Action Duels mix running, jumping, and grabbing Action Cards from the field with normal card game strategy.

Visual Details to Watch

  • How each dimension uses color to show its mood.
  • How Yuya’s dragons change as the story goes on.
  • Posters and signs in the background of city scenes.
  • Differences between “fun” duels and war-like duels.

Even if you ignore the card text, you can follow a lot of the story just by watching colors, expressions, and posing.
