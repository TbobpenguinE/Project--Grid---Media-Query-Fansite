Episode Overview

This page highlights a few important parts of Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V. You can change the episode names and comments later to match your own favorites.

  1. Early: Yuya develops his Entertainment Duel style.
  2. Middle: The group travels to other dimensions.
  3. Late: The truth behind the war and the “four Yuyas”.

Episode 1: First Big Duel

Yuya is pushed into a serious duel in front of a crowd. It sets up his dream of making people smile through dueling.

  • Focus: Yuya Sakaki
  • Theme: Performing under pressure

Early School Episode

We see You Show Duel School and why it looks “weak” from the outside but actually supports creative duel styles.

  • Focus: Yuya and Yuzu
  • Theme: Believing in a small school

The Pendulum Reveal

Pendulum Summoning appears and changes how duels work. It becomes the main mechanic that defines ARC-V.

  • Focus: New summon mechanic
  • Theme: Change and potential

Synchro Dimension Arc

The cast ends up in a city where duels affect rankings and freedom. The system is strict and punishes “losers”.

  • Focus: New allies and rivals
  • Theme: Freedom vs. control

Emotional Dragon Duel

Yuya’s dragons reflect his emotions. When he loses control, the duel feels dangerous, not just flashy.

  • Focus: Yuya and his dragons
  • Theme: Fear of your own power

Late-Series Turning Point

Different dimensions, different versions of Yuya, and old plans all collide in one big set of duels.

  • Focus: Core main cast
  • Theme: Identity and choice
